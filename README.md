## ✨ Overview

**D Table** is the first product being launched under **D Company**.

The app is designed to make restaurant reservations in **Trinidad and Tobago** easier, faster, and more organized by giving users a modern platform to:

- discover restaurants
- browse restaurant details
- request and manage reservations
- receive confirmations and reminders

At the same time, D Table helps restaurants move away from scattered booking methods such as phone calls, WhatsApp messages, Instagram DMs, and manual reservation books.

---

## 🏢 About D Company

**D Company** is the parent brand behind a planned ecosystem of Caribbean-focused digital service applications.

Its purpose is to build simple, trusted, and modern platforms for services people use in everyday life across Trinidad and Tobago and, eventually, the wider Caribbean.

### Planned ecosystem examples
- **D Table** — restaurant reservations
- **D Ticket** — event ticketing
- future service-based apps under the same recognizable brand

---

## 🎯 Vision

To make **D Table** the go-to platform for restaurant discovery and table reservations in Trinidad and Tobago, while laying the foundation for a broader digital ecosystem under **D Company**.

---

## 🧩 Problem Being Solved

Restaurant reservations in Trinidad and Tobago are often managed through:

- phone calls
- WhatsApp messages
- Instagram DMs
- inconsistent manual systems

This creates issues such as:

- missed reservations
- slow responses
- booking confusion
- poor organization
- lost customers for restaurants

**D Table** aims to solve this by offering a centralized, user-friendly reservation system for both diners and restaurants.

## 📱 Core Experience

D Table is being designed to serve two sides of the platform.

### For Customers
- discover restaurants across Trinidad and Tobago
- view restaurant details, hours, and location
- reserve tables quickly
- manage upcoming and past reservations
- receive confirmations and reminders

### For Restaurants
- manage reservations digitally
- control availability
- improve booking organization
- reduce missed calls and manual errors
- gain more visibility to customers

## ✅ Why Flutter

Flutter was chosen because it allows D Table to be built from a **shared codebase** for both **Android** and **iOS**, which is ideal for an early-stage product that needs to launch efficiently while still feeling polished.

### Benefits
- faster cross-platform development
- shared UI and business logic
- easier long-term maintenance
- strong support for modern app interfaces
- scalable foundation for future D Company apps
